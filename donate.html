Thank You For Your Support

The reason the Dream Center is making such a huge impact on families AT NO COST is due in part through the collaborative support of donors, local churches, and teaming up with other organizations that provide needed services
Donate Now


We are currently in the process of moving locations! Would you (or your small group) prayerfully consider giving toward the new 35,000 square foot Dream Center that will house 75-125 women and children in crisis and provide them an opportunity to start over? A gift of $1,000 or Sponsoring a Room for $12,000 will greatly impact our community! For more information contact Gail Gustafson at (731) 613-0741 or Stephanie Laffoon at (731) 267-9016.


All donations are appreciated, but here are some of the current items we need the most.

  • Fresh fruit and vegetables
  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Canned goods
  • Gently used clothing

We are committed to respecting the privacy of our donors.
The Dream Center will not sell, rent or lease your personal information to other organizations. We assure you that the identity of all our donors will be kept confidential. It is not our desire to send unwanted mail to our donors. Please contact us if you wish to be removed from our mailing list.


The Dream center will use contact information of donors for the following purposes only
  • Distribute receipts for donations
  • Send thank you notes to donors
  • Inform donors about upcoming fundraising activities and events
